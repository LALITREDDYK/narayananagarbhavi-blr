Program Overview

The CO SUPER CHAINA Program by Narayana is a high-intensity, result-driven academic framework tailored for students aspiring to crack IIT-JEE and other top-tier competitive exams.

Program Structure

Phase I

Syllabus Completion (12 Months)

  • Complete Class 11 & 12 syllabus
  • Aligned with IIT-JEE standards
  • 48 Weekly Tests
  • 12 Cumulative Tests
  • Cross-topic mastery

Phase II

Intensive Revision (14 Weeks)

  • 15 Practice Test Modules
  • IIT-JEE style papers
  • 15 CTMs / CTAs / CETs
  • Improve speed & accuracy
  • Exam readiness boost

Phase III

Final Sprint (12-14 Weeks)

  • 20 GTMs (Phase III)
  • 20 GTMs (Phase II)
  • 10 GTAs
  • 20 Local CETs
  • High-impact final revision

Why Choose CO SUPER CHAINA?

With a strategic blend of syllabus mastery, continuous evaluation and focused revision, the CO SUPER CHAINA Program equips students with the confidence, competence and exam temperament required to excel in IIT-JEE and beyond.
