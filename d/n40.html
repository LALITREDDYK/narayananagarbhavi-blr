Programme Overview

A focused, high-precision academic system designed for students aiming to secure top state medical merit ranks and admission into leading government medical colleges.

Integrated coaching covering CBSE or State Board + NEET
CDF Modules (Concept, Definition & Formulae)
Micro-Schedules for daily, weekly and monthly milestones
State Evaluation Tests (SETs)
N-40 Accuracy + Speed Drills

Phase III

Final Performance Boost (3 Months)

  • 12 Cumulative Tests
  • Crash revision schedule
  • Advanced performance analytics
